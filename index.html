Raising standards in elearning

An open-source competency framework designed for elearning professionals - across the range of roles, including:

  • Design
  • Development
  • Consulting
  • Systems
  • Product design
  • Project management

Using this framework

The framework is based on 30 areas, each containing 3 levels:

  • Level 1: Foundation
  • Level 2: Practitioner
  • Level 3: Advanced

The levels are an indication of the amount of responsibility you may have in this area.
